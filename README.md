🛡️ Enhanced Slime Invader
Slime Invader is a fast-paced, top-down action survival game where strategy meets skill. Battle waves of relentless enemies, collect precious gems, and survive long enough to face the legendary Magma Slime Boss.

Between waves, visit the village shops to bolster your defenses or enhance your offensive capabilities. Will you prioritize a heavy shield, or go for maximum damage?

🚀 Key Features
Dynamic Wave System: Survive escalating waves of monsters. The tension rises as the timer ticks down to the next wave!

The Shop Hub: Enter the Shield Shop or Ability Hut to spend your hard-earned gems on permanent upgrades.

Epic Boss Encounters: Reach Level 5 to trigger the boss fight. An animated, high-stakes battle against a giant flaming magma slime.

Pixel Art Aesthetic: Features custom-animated sprites, seamless tiled environments, and a gooey, slime-themed UI.

Advanced Physics: Smooth sliding collisions and wave-based spawning logic ensure a polished gameplay feel.

🕹️ How to Play
Survive: Move through the arena and take out enemies to earn gems.

Loot: Collect gems before they disappear!

Upgrade: When a wave ends, head to the shop. Walk up to the huts to purchase weapon or shield upgrades.

Conquer: Defeat the boss to prove your worth in the arena.

Controls:

Move: WASD / Arrow Keys

Aim/Shoot: Mouse

Shop Interaction: Walk into the shop zones during wave breaks.

Pause: [P] key

🛠️ Technical Details
Built with C# and Silk.NET/SDL2, this project showcases:

Efficient 2D rendering with texture slicing for animations.

Seamless tilemap generation and scrolling backgrounds.

Custom UI logic with pixel-perfect collision detection.

Unsafe memory management for high-performance sprite handling.
